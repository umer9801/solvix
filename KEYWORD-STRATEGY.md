## 🎨 Content Optimization Guidelines

### Title Tag Formula
```
[Primary Keyword] | [Secondary Benefit] | Solvix Core
```

**Examples**:
- "Web Development Company Canada | Custom Website Development | Solvix Core"
- "AI Automation Services Canada | Custom AI Development | Solvix Core"
- "SEO Services Canada | Digital Marketing Agency | Solvix Core"

### Meta Description Formula
```
[Service Type] in [Location]. [Key Benefits]. [Social Proof]. [Call to Action].
```

**Examples**:
- "Professional web development company in Canada. Custom websites, Next.js apps, ecommerce solutions. Serving Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal. Get a free quote."
- "Leading AI automation services in Canada. Custom AI development, machine learning, intelligent automation. Transform your business today."

### H1 Tag Formula
```
[Primary Keyword with Emphasis]
```

**Examples**:
- "**Web** Development Services Canada"
- "**AI** Automation & Machine Learning"
- "**SEO** Services & Digital Marketing"

## 📈 Ranking Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)
**Goal**: Get indexed and establish presence
- Focus on long-tail keywords (lower competition)
- Build quality content around primary keywords
- Optimize technical SEO
- Submit to search engines

**Target Keywords**:
- Long-tail variations with "canada"
- City-specific keywords (Toronto, Vancouver)
- Service + industry combinations

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-6)
**Goal**: Improve rankings for secondary keywords
- Increase content production
- Build quality backlinks
- Optimize existing content
- Add location pages

**Target Keywords**:
- Secondary keywords for each service
- Semantic variations
- Question-based keywords

### Phase 3: Dominance (Months 7-12)
**Goal**: Rank for primary competitive keywords
- Aggressive content marketing
- Authority building
- Featured snippet optimization
- Comprehensive link building

**Target Keywords**:
- Primary keywords (high competition)
- Branded keywords
- Industry leadership terms

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Keyword Cannibalization**: Avoid targeting same keyword on multiple pages
2. **Semantic SEO**: Use related terms naturally in content
3. **User Intent**: Match content to search intent (informational vs transactional)
4. **Local Signals**: Include city names, provinces, "Canada" naturally
5. **Long-tail Focus**: Easier to rank, higher conversion rates
6. **Question Keywords**: Great for FAQ schema and featured snippets
7. **Trending Topics**: Monitor AI and automation keyword trends
8. **Bilingual**: Consider French keywords for Quebec market
